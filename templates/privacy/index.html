What this means

Your IP address is a number used on the Internet that identifies your network. It allows us to determine your city/town/region and ISP (Internet Service Provider), as well as perform abuse checks if we have reason to believe you are visiting this site maliciously.

Your User-Agent string allows us to determine your browser and operating system.
